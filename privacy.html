Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 April 2026

Overview

PrePurr ("we", "our") builds test preparation apps. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

Data We Collect

Our apps store all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. Your test scores, progress, and preferences stay on your device.

Analytics

We do not use any third-party analytics or tracking services. We do not collect usage data, device identifiers, or location information.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy governs these transactions.

Images

Some explanatory images are loaded from Wikimedia Commons when you answer a question. These requests go directly to Wikimedia's servers. We do not proxy, track, or log these requests.

Children's Privacy

Our apps do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Our apps contain no user accounts, social features, or data collection mechanisms.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this URL.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email prepurr.apps@gmail.com.
