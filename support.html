Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open the app, go to Profile, and tap "Restore Purchases". Your purchase is linked to your Apple ID.

Are these the real test questions?

These are practice questions based on the official "Life in the United Kingdom" study guide. They cover the same topics and format as the real test.

How many questions do I need to pass?

The real test has 24 questions and you need at least 18 correct (75%) to pass. Our mock tests follow the same format.

My progress was reset after an update

Occasionally, app updates require refreshing the question bank to add new content. Your test scores are preserved, but the question data is re-loaded.
